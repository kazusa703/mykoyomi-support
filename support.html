Frequently Asked Questions

How does MYKOYOMI work?

When you press "Start", that day becomes your January 1st. Track your daily habits, and your personal calendar advances each day. If you miss too many days (based on your settings), your year ends and you can start fresh.

What happens when my year ends?

You'll see a summary of your year, including which habit caused it to end. You can take a break (refresh period) and start a new year whenever you're ready. Your Year number increases each time.

What happens if I complete 365 days?

Congratulations! You'll see a special celebration with confetti and earn a completion badge. Your completed year is marked with a gold star in your history.

How do I sync my data across devices?

MYKOYOMI uses iCloud to sync your data. Make sure you're signed in to the same Apple ID on all devices and that iCloud sync is enabled in the app's Settings.

How do I remove ads?

Go to Settings > Remove Ads and make a one-time purchase. Once purchased, ads are permanently removed on all devices using the same Apple ID. You can restore your purchase at any time.

How do I delete my data?

Deleting the app removes all local data. To remove iCloud data, go to your device's Settings > Apple ID > iCloud > Manage Storage and remove MYKOYOMI's data.
